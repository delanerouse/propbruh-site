// THE METHOD

Three Pillars. One Framework.

The Prop Bruh Method isn't a single setup. It's a layered framework where three battle-tested approaches work together to give you a reason to be in every trade, and a reason to stay out of every bad one.

01

SphinxFib

Precision entries at key fibonacci levels. The primary framework I run day to day, combining fib confluence with execution triggers to catch high-probability reversals and continuations.

Method credit: Sphinx Trading on YouTube
02

ORB

Multiple daily opening range breakout sessions. The rule set that built the discipline: session-based setups, defined range, measured targets, stop-trading after two consecutive losses.

03

Market Structure

The structural layer underneath every trade. Clean horizontal levels built from previous day/session opens, closes, highs, lows and key levels. The context that makes every setup smarter.

Method credit: Jimmy at Trading Decoded
